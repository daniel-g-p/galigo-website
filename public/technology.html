Our Technology

7 HD Cameras

Equipped with 7 high-definition cameras with superior shutter speed, the eHermes receiver has a 360° field of view to take images of its urban surroundings at high volume.

GNSS Antenna

The built-in global navigation satellite system antenna enables an unprecedented geolocation accuracy of 30cm, 6 times the precision that was previously attainable.

Solar Panels

Thanks to its integrated solar panels, the eHermes receiver sources its energy from renewable sources, giving it an autonomy of up to 12 hours.

Adjustment Pivot

The adjustment pivot allows for a fine-tuned positioning and orientation of the receiver so that it captures everything it needs to capture to perfection.

Locking Mechanism

A robust locking mechanism that combines strong magnetic attachment with trustworthy suction cups ensures the secure and flexible deployment of the receiver on any urban vehicle.
